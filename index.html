My Road to Software Engineer

Hi! I'm Sherry Bai, an aspiring full-time Python developer. My journey began in the bustling world of Wall Street as a financial analyst. It was an interesting start, but I often found myself caught up in repetitive tasks.

Then there comes the magic day when I wrote my first VB program in an Excel spreadsheet in 2016, which saved me an hour daily. I felt like a magician, unlocking a new realm of possibilities.

Driven by this revelation, I immersed myself in the world of Python and SQL, focusing on streamlining workflows and enhancing data analysis processes. My efforts soon led me to a rewarding role as a data analyst in 2018. Over the next five years, I ascended through the ranks to become the lead data analyst for Arknights, a game with a global user base of 10 billion. In addition, I devoted my spare time to developing websites, web crawlers, and indie games.

This year marks a pivotal transition for me as I venture into full-time Python development. I'm currently seeking new opportunities and am eager to connect with like-minded professionals and potential employers. Feel free to reach out to me at sherrybaibai@163.com!
